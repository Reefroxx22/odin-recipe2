Deviled Eggs

This is a good snack to keep on hand. Lots of nutrients, no sugar, and keeps you from reaching for a sugary snack. The only down side is that it is not filling.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Red Wine Vinegar
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Optional: Bacon
  • Optional: Green Onion

I usually do 4 eggs at a time. Hard boil the eggs by placing the eggs in cold water, bringing to a boil and immediately taking them off the heat. Let them continue to cook/sit in the hot water for another 11-12 minutes then stop the cooking process by running them under cold water. This keeps the egg yolks from getting that weird green/black patina.

Halve the eggs and combine all the yolks in a cup. Add a spoonful of mayo (maybe a tbsp), roughly half spoonful of mustard, a drizzle of red wine vinegar, some salt, pepper, and smoked paprika and mash it all together with a fork. Start on the stingy side w/these ingredients and add in proportion until you get the creamy texture you want. Ease up on the salt if you plan to add bacon.

It's best if you can let them sit overnight to let all the flavors meld, but then they're ready!
