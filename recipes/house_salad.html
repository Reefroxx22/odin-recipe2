Michele's House Salad Recipe

This is a great "reset salad" that helps bring your balance back after many days of eating junk. The olive oil seems to have an anti-inflamatory effect, and the electrolytes, fiber, vitamins and minerals all help bring your skin and digestion back into a comfortable state you can be proud of.

Ingredients:

  • Greens. For convenience this can come in a spring mix. Buying a head of lettuce takes a little more work but lasts longer and probably has more in tact nutrients
  • Shredded carrot
  • Mixed nuts. Any mix of sliced almonds, pecan pieces, pumpkin seeds or even a small amount of sunflower seeds
  • Olive oil
  • Red wine or balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper
  • For a sweeter salad, pomegrante or raspberries
  • For a savory salad, smoked salmon or tuna salad

Mix the greens, carrot, nuts, oil, vinegar, salt, pepper, and fruit. For a savory salad mix everything but the fruit and put the fish on top. Voila!
